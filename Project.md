Key Insights

Chicago generates the highest profit margin among all cities.

Coffee Beverages are the most profitable product category.

Product COF001 contributes the highest profit margin.

Brooklyn branch shows the best overall performance.

Some products and branches have low profit, indicating areas for improvement.
